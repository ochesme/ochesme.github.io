Answer:

The word is "ukit". It is a Filipino word that means "to carve" or "carving". The word originated from the old Malayan language, which was spoken in the Philippines before the arrival of the Spanish colonizers.


4. 20. Which is NOT an example of Maranao art? A. Torogan B. Sarimanok C. Okit D. Tabih​


Answer:

B.Sarimanok

Explanation:

The Sarimanok is the legendary bird that has become an ubiquitous symbol of Maranao art. It is depicted as a fowl with colorful wings and feathered tail, holding a fish on its beak or talons. The head is profusely decorated with scroll, leaf, and spiral motifs. It is said to be a symbol of good fortune

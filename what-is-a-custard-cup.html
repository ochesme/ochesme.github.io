24. A container holds some cups of sugar. Grace used 1/4 of the sugar for baking some cakes and 1/3 of what was left for cooking custard. Now, the container has only 4 cups of sugar left. How many cups of sugar were in the container at the start?​


Answer:

4-(1/4+1/3)

4-(7/12)

= 41/12 or 3 5/12

ANALYSIS:

Sinabi sa problem na si Grace ay ginamit ang 1/4 ng sugar sa pagbe-bake, at 1/3 ng 1/4 na iyon ay ginamit sa paggawa ng custard. So, meaning, ang binawas lang sa sugar ay 1/4. Kaya naman, ang solution sa tanong ay;

√4 - 1/4.

SOLUTION:

[tex]4 - \frac{1}{4} = \frac{(4 \times 4) - (1 \times 1)}{(1 \times 4)} \\ = \frac{16 - 1}{4} \\ = \frac{15}{4} \: or \: 3 \frac{3}{4} [/tex]

FINAL ANSWER:

Si Grace ay gumamit lamang ng 1/4 ng sugar kaya naman 3 3/4 pa ang natirang asukal.

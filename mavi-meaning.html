23. Mavie has a rectangular sheet of paper with length of 18cm and width of 12cm. She cuts out a smalrectangular piece measuring 6cm by 4cm at one corner of the paper. Find the remaining area of thepaper.​


Answer:

192 cm²

Step-by-step explanation:

To get the remaining area, you need to subtract the area of the original paper (18×12) and the cutted rectangular piece (6×4).

Area = L×W

Original Paper = 18×12 = 216 cm²

Cutted Paper = 6×4 = 24 cm²

Subtract.

216 - 24 = 192 cm²

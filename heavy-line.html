28. Under line the determiner and mention its kind the heavy rain flattened their sunflowers.


Determiners

Determiner is a word that introduces a noun. It is always comes before a noun, not after, and it also comes before any other adjectives used to describe the noun. For example in the sentence below:

The bunny went home.I ate the chocolate ice cream for dessert.The metal cans are recyclable.

In the sentence

The heavy rain flattened their sunflowers.

The determiner in this sentence is "the" it was a kind of definite article. The article “the” is considered as a definite article because it refers to a particular noun that is mentioned in the text, while the articles “a” and “an” are called indefinite articles because they point out to a class of noun mentioned in the sentence.

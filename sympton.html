2. what are the symptons of covid 19​


Answer:

COVID-19 affects different people in different ways. Most infected people will develop mild to moderate illness and recover without hospitalization.

Most common symptoms:

fever dry cough tiredness

Less common symptoms:

aches and pains sore throat diarrhea conjunctivitis headache loss of taste or smell a rash on skin, or discoloration of fingers or toes

Serious symptoms:

difficulty breathing or shortness of breath chest pain or pressure loss of speech or movement

Seek immediate medical attention if you have serious symptoms. Always call before visiting your doctor or health facility.

People with mild symptoms who are otherwise healthy should manage their symptoms at home.

On average it takes 5–6 days from when someone is infected with the virus for symptoms to show, however it can take up to 14 days.

10. The ratio of ducks to chicken in the farm is 3:5.The total number of chickens and duck together is 72 if 6 chicken has a sympton of a flu and had to removed from the farm,what is the new ratio of the ducks to chickens?


Answer:

9 : 13

Step-by-step explanation:

3 + 5 = 8 (simplified representation of the total number of ducks and chickens at the farm)

72/8 = 9 (representation of how many ducks/chickens there would be if we divided them into groups based on the ratio)

9(5) = 45 (total number of chickens)

45 - 6 = 39 (number of chickens after the chickens that got the flu were removed)

9(3) = 27 (number of ducks)

27 : 39 = 9 : 13 (simplified ratio of the current number of ducks and chickens)

16. The ratio of ducks to chicken in the farm is 3:5.The total no. of chickens and ducks together is 72.If 6 chickens have shown symptons of flu and had to be removed from the farm,what is the new ratio of ducks to chickens?


Determine first the number of chicken.
3x + 5x = 72
8x = 72
8x/8 = 72/8
x = 9

ducks = 3x = 3(9) = 27
chicken = 5x = 5(9) = 45
Subtract 6 chickens with flu from the number of chickens.
45 - 6 = 39
There are 27 ducks and 39 chickens left.
In ratio, 27:39, since the two numbers are divisible by 3 then divide 3 from 27 and 39. The answer is 9:13. This is now the new ratio of ducks to the chicken.

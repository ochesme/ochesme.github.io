In terms of parenting styles, I believe that the authoritative parenting style will enable a child to differentiate appropriate from inappropriate behaviors. This style encourages a child to think for themselves, to consider the consequences of their actions, and to make their own decisions. By providing the right balance of guidance and autonomy, the authoritative parenting style can help a child to develop the skills and confidence they need to make good choices and to become a responsible and well-rounded adult.

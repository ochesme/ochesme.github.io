20. John receives PhP10,000 as monthly salary. As an agent, his monthly quota is PhP50,000 worth of beauty products for which he gets 5% commission on all sales beyond his monthly quota. If he sold PhP75,000 worth of beauty products this month, what is his total salary for this month?


Answer:

Php.11,250.00

Step-by-step explanation:

First, subtract his monthly quota to the total amount he sold this month.

[tex] \tt{} = 75000 - 50000[/tex]

[tex] \tt{} = 25000[/tex]

Compute for the commission.

[tex] \tt{} = 5 \% \times 25000[/tex]

[tex] \tt{} = 1250[/tex]

Add his the commission to his monthly salary.

[tex] \tt{} = 10000 + 1250[/tex]

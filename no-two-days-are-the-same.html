15. To finish a certaon job in 8 days,6 workers are needed.If it is required to finish the same job in two days in advance how many workers have to work?


Inverse proportion/variation: If one variable increases, the other decreases.

Number of days, y: 8

Number of workers, x: 6

Inverse Variation equation:

y = k/x

Find k, the constant of variation:

8 = k/6

k = (6)(8)

k = 48

Find the number of workers, x, if the number of days are reduced by 2.

(8-2) = 48/x

x = 48/6

x = 8 workers

ANSWER: 8 workers have to work if the same job is to be finished two days in advance or in 6 days.

rate of the one man = 1/20

other man can do = x

[tex] \frac{1}{20} + \frac{1}{x} = \frac{1}{10} [/tex]

the LCD is 20x.Then multiply the LCD to the both sides to remove the fractions.

[tex]20x( \frac{1}{20} + \frac{1}{x}) = ( \frac{1}{10} ) 20x[/tex]

29. five painters paint 1800m2 of wall in two days. In how many days. two painters, with the same productivity, painted 1080m2 of wall? ​


Answer:

3 days

Step-by-step explanation:

First let's analyze the question:

We have that 5 painters paint a total of 1800 m² of wall in two days, considering the same productivity for each painter, we have that in two days a painter is able to paint:

1800 / 5 = 360 m² of wall in two days

Therefore, in just one day a painter paints 360 / 2 = 180 m²

The exercise asks how many days two painters with the same productivity paint 1080 m² and as we know that a single painter is able to paint 180 m² per day, we have that two painters paint a day:

180 × 2 = 360 m²

So 1080 / 360 = 3

Therefore, two painters, with the same productivity, painted 1080 m² of wall in 3 days.

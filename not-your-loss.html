10. the loss of muscle function in part of your body?​


Answer:

Total muscle function loss, or paralysis, affects your entire body. It's often seen in people with severe spinal cord injuries.

If a loss of muscle function affects both the top half and bottom half of your body, it's called quadriplegia.

If it affects only the bottom half of your body, it's called paraplegia.

23. what interest rate will the given principal P=150,000 earn the given simple interest I=1,000 in the given term t= 2years?​


RATE OF INTEREST

What interest rate will the given principal P = 150,000 earn the given simple interest I = 1,000 in the given term t = 2 years?

FORMULA:

[tex]\boxed{r = \frac{Is}{Pt}}[/tex]

where:

r = is the rate

Is = is the simple interest

P = is the principal

t = is the time

SOLUTION:

r = 1,000/(150,000)(2)

r = 1,000/300,000

[tex]\boxed{r = 0.003}[/tex] or 0.3%

ANSWER:

The interest rate will be 0.3...%

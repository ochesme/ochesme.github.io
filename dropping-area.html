11. a bomb was dropped in an area where rebels were hiding. the perimeter of the damage area with 480 meters and with was 15 meters. what is the length of the damage area? how to solve these problem please​


Answer:

The length of the damage area is 225 meters.

Step-by-step explanation:

The formula of the perimeter of a rectangle is:

P = 2L + 2W

Given:

P = 480 m

W = 15 m

The unknown is:

L = ?

Solution

First, let's derive the formula of the perimeter:

P = 2L + 2W

2L = P - 2W

L = (P - 2W) / 2

Now, substitute the given numbers to the derived equation,

L = (P - 2W) / 2

L = (480 - 2(15)) / 2

L = (480 - 30) / 2

L = 450 / 2

L = 225

Therefore, the final answer is:

L = 225 m

30. 22. Mister Garcia wants to customize his own bath tub. He wants the length of the bath tub to be 9 ft. longer than the width. If the area of the bath tub is 90 ft2, what are the dimensions of the bath tub?​


Answer:

Dimensions:

Length = 15ftWidth = 6ft

Step-by-step explanation:

Formula: A=lw

Given:

l=(x+9)w=xA=90

> 90=(x+9)x

> 90=x²+9x

> x²+9x-90=0

> x²+15x-6x-90=0

> x(x+15)-6(x+15)

> (x+15)(x-6)

> x=-15, x=6

Evaluate 6 by x+9

> x+9

> 6+9

> 15 is the length and 6 is the width

Answer:

The dimensions are 6ft. and 15ft

6ft. x 15ft = 90 ft2

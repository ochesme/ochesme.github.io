Why was the shadow the shortest that time?--The bigger angle, the longer the shadow. At noon, the Sun is directly overhead. This makes the smallest angle with the objects and so creates the shortest shadows.3. What is the length of your shadow in the morning?--It depends on u 4. What is the length of your shadow in the afternoon?--It depends on u5. What can you say about the length of shadows during the day?--When we are outside on a sunny day, we can see how our shadows change throughout the day.

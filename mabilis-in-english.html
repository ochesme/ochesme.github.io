Answer:

1. 103

The total angle of a pentagon is 540 degrees. We will add all of the angles then subtract by the total angles.
x = 540 - (117 + 100 + 105 + 115)

x = 103
2. 135

In a regular octagon, all of the interior angles are equal to each other. One interior angle is 135 degrees.
3. 130

The total exterior angle is 360, we will only subtract the 230 angles to the total exterior angle.
x = 360 - 230

x = 130

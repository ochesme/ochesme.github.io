Bending.

Bouncing.

Pushing.

Rocking.

Turning.

Weight transfer.

Movement occurring above a stationary base; movement of the body around its own axis (also called axial movement, which includes bending, stretching, pushing, pulling, bouncing, swinging, shaking and twisting).

The movements just need to travel from one place to another. Non-locomotor movement is “movement that moves around the axis of the body (the spine) rather than movement which takes the body through space.” Non-locomotor movement is anchored movement. It stays in one place.

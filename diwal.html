1. Why diwal is called angel wings?​


Diwal or Angel Wings Clam Facts: Diwal in Hiligaynon means “sticking out tongue” and the shellfish probably got it's Ilonggo name because it's “feet” sticks out of it's shell like a tongue when alive. It's English name was due to the fact that it's shells are like angel wings when fully opened.

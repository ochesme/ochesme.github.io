3. fine the5th term 13th term in 6 eometrik sequence 2,4,8,16


Answer:

a5 = 16 & a13= 48

Step-by-step explanation:

an = a1r(n-1)

r = 4/2 = 2

a1 = 2

n = 5

Finding the 5th term:

a5 = (2)(2)(5-1)

a5 = (4)(4)

a5 = 16

Finding the 13th term:

a13 = (2)(2)(13-1)

a13 = (4)(12)

a13 = 48

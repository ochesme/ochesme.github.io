24. avoid wishing happy birthday or merry christmas to jehovah's witnesses this is true or false​


Answer:

true

Explanation:

i'm a Jehovah's witness, we don't celebrate birthdays and christmas.

answer

A witness wont expect you to wish them birthday wishes. They won't want them either. You don't have to feel bad to not recognise their birthday. A witness wont take offence if you do wish them a happy birthday, they will just feel aware that you do not have an understanding of their beliefs.

explanation

Because Jehovah's Witnesses do not celebrate birthdays, it is offensive to say “Merry Christmas” to a Jehovah's Witness, as well as “Happy Birthday.” Both are disrespectful greetings. There are no special greetings for the following occasions: Jehovah's Witnesses do not celebrate any holidays, so even a generic card would be inappropriate.

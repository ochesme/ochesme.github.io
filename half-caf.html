Answer:

∠CAF is the obtuse angle. Other than that, ∠EAC, ∠BAD, and ∠BAF are also obtuse angles. Not so sure about ∠FAD and ∠FAE but they seem to be obtuse too.

Step-by-step explanation:

Obtuse angles are angles that are higher than 90 degrees. Just note that the opposite of obtuse is 'acute'

Tip to remember why an angle is called in such way:

Always think of the keyword 'cute' in 'acute' which will mean that the angle is smaller than 90 degrees, where 'small' is 'cute'

Having this in mind, you'll know the opposite of acute: obtuse. Now for obtuse, think of the keyword 'obese' for 'obtuse' which will mean that the angle is bigger than 90 degrees, where 'obese' is 'big'

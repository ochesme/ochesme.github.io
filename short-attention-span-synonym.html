28. Effects of Social Media to Young Adolescents' Attention Span at School​


Answer:

The effects of social media on young adolescents' attention span at school can be both positive and negative. On the one hand, social media can provide a platform for students to connect with each other and share ideas, which can help them stay engaged in class discussions. On the other hand, it can also be a distraction from learning if students are spending too much time on their phones or computers during class. Additionally, research has shown that excessive use of social media is linked to lower academic performance due to decreased focus and concentration. Therefore, it is important for teachers and parents to monitor how much time students are spending on their devices during school hours in order to ensure that they are staying focused on their studies.

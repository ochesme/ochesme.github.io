4. what is the work done by a horse that exerts an applied force of 100 on the sleigh , if the harness makes an angle of 30 with the ground and the sleigh moves 30 m across a flat level ice surface​


[tex]\tt{\huge{\blue{Explanation:}}}[/tex]

The work done by an object exerting a force F at an angle θ and at a distance d is given by

[tex]\boxed{W = Fd \: \cos \theta}[/tex]

where:

W = work

F = force

d = distance

θ = angle of the force with the horizontal

[tex]\tt{\huge{\red{Solution:}}}[/tex]

Solving for W

W = Fd cos θ

W = (100 N)(30 m)(cos 30°)

[tex]\boxed{W = \text{2,598.08 J}}[/tex]

Therefore, the work done is 2,598.08 J.

1. Table Tennis

-Is a sport in which two or four players hit a lightweight ball, also known as the ping-pong ball, back and forth across a table using small rackets. The game takes place on a hard table divided by a net. Except for the initial serve, the rules are generally as follows: players must allow a ball played toward them to bounce one time on their side of the table and must return it so that it bounces on the opposite side at least once. A point is scored when a player fails to return the ball within the rules. Play is fast and demands quick reactions.

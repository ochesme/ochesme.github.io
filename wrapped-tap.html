1. What is the description or meaning of the WRAPPED TAP OR TEE JOINT​


Answer:

Wrapped Tap or Tee Joint --- Used on large solid conductors where a heavy tap wire is difficult to wrap around the main wire. Used strips of stranded wires to wrap around the T-formed solid wires to connect and paste them together.

8. 8 wrapping style in wrap text​


Answer:

Square wrapping

The most common wrapping style is Square, which means the text wraps around a rectangular box (called a “bounding box”) that encloses the image.

Tight wrapping

Tight wrapping is similar to square wrapping, but instead of following the line of the bounding box, it follows the shape of the image.

Through wrapping

The least understood wrapping option is Through.

Top and Bottom wrapping

Sometimes you want an image to be on a line by itself. 

Behind Text and In Front of Text

The last two wrapping styles don’t actually cause the text to wrap around the image.

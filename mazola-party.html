2. Every person at a party of twenty-eight people said hello to each of the other people at the parry exactly once, how many "hello's" were said at the party?


Number of people saying hellos to each other exactly once (no repetition): 2

Number of people, n = 28

To exclude the person saying hello (because he does say hello to himself): n-1

Equation:

Number of hellos = (n/2) (n - 1)

Number of hellos = (28/2)(28-1)

Number of hellos = (14)(27)

Number of hellos = 378 hellos

ANSWER: 378

22. function of madolas farming tool​


Answer:

A madolas is a farming tool used in the Philippines. It is a type of rake that is used to cultivate the soil and remove weeds. The function of a madolas is to help prepare the soil for planting, making it more loose and aerated so that seeds can be planted and will have a better chance of growing. It is also used to remove weeds, which can compete with crops for nutrients, water, and light, and can reduce their yield. By using a madolas, farmers can improve the health of their crops and increase their productivity.

24. There are 14 party hats at a party and 4/7 of them are red. How many party hats that are not red are at the party? ​


Answer:

6

Step-by-step explanation:

Solution

What is the problem asking?

 -  Number of party hats that are not white

What information do you have?

  - 4 out of every 7 party hats (or 4/7) of the 14 party hats are white.

   This means that 3 out of every 7 party hats (or 3/7) of the 14 party hats are not white.

What operation do you use?

 - We are looking for a fraction of a group which means multiplication.

Equation

14 * 3/7 = 6

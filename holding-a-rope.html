1. ________ is a force that is present in the ropes that hold a swing.​


Answer:

Tension

Explanation:

Tension is a contact force transmitted through a rope, string, wire or something similar when forces on opposite ends are pulling on it. For example, a tire swing hanging from a tree causes tension in the rope holding it to the branch.

The centripetal force is the force required to keep the ball rotating in a circle, by providing it the necessary centripetal acceleration.

23. bakit ginawa ni Jacques Louis David yung the death of marat​


Answer:

(English)

Jacques Louis David painted "The Death of Marat" to commemorate the death of his friend, Jean-Paul Marat, who was a prominent French revolutionary assassinated in his bathtub by a political opponent. David, who was a supporter of the French Revolution, used his artistic talents to create an idealized portrait of Marat as a martyr for the cause of the revolution.

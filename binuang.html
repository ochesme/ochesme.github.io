How to easily find out the moral message of a story

1.First, the moral message is at the end of the short story.

For short stories primarily fables, the moral is usually stated at the end of the story. Of course because this story has a target audience that is still in the stage of learning to read stories.

2.Second, the moral message can be found in the problems and solutions throughout the story.

The moral message can be found in the problems faced by the characters in the story. The way to find it is to identify the problem in the story. Next, see how the problem is solved. So, the moral message will be read.

3.Third, the moral message is known from the characters.

The moral message can be found from the characters in the story. To find out the character of a character, you have to see how he reacts to problems, how he talks, and how he gets along. So that way the implied moral message will be found.

30. Which statement best summarizes the time period after Hitler took control of Germany? It was a chaotic time in which Hitler created and enforced many new policies. It was a peaceful time because people united behind Hitler’s new policies. It was a rebuilding time in which Hitler made only a few drastic changes. It was a confusing time because Hitler was slow to create new policies.


Answer:

The statement "It was a chaotic time in which Hitler created and enforced many new policies" best summarizes the time period after Hitler took control of Germany. Hitler quickly consolidated power and established a totalitarian regime in which he implemented numerous policies aimed at purging "undesirable" elements from German society, expanding German territory through aggressive foreign policy, and rearming the military in preparation for war. The period was marked by repression, persecution, and violence against perceived enemies of the state, including Jews, communists, socialists, homosexuals, and others.

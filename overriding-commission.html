12. P100,000 on top of his P2,500 weekly salary. In addition to that, he has an override commission of ½% on his men's sales. How much is his gross pay for the month if his sales amounted to P200,000 and the people under him got P475,000?pe help please ​


Answer:

The override commission is calculated by multiplying the sales of the people under him (P475,000) by 0.5% which is equal to P2,375.

So, his gross pay for the month is calculated by adding his weekly salary, the P100,000 bonus and the override commission, which is:

(P2,500 x 4) + P100,000 + P2,375 = P11,000 + P100,000 + P2,375 = P113,375

That would be his gross pay for the month.

Step-by-step explanation:

1. Calculate the weekly salary: P2,500 x 4 = P10,000 (This is the amount he earns every week)

2. Add the bonus: P10,000 + P100,000 = P110,000 (This is the amount he earns every week plus the bonus)

3. Calculate the override commission: P475,000 x 0.5% = P2,375 (This is the amount he earns from the sales of the people under him)

4. Add the commission to the salary and bonus: P110,000 + P2,375 = P112,375 (This is the total amount he earns from his salary, bonus, and commission)

So, the gross pay for the month would be P112,375

30. 1 Jose makes a basic monthly salary of P12,700. He is entitled to a 3% commission on the first P300,000 of his personal sales. Anything above P300,000 will grant him a 4.5% commission more. In addition to that, he gets an override commission of %2% on the sales of the people under his supervision. If Jose sold P720,000 worth of items this month, and his men sold P1,350,000, what is his total income for the month including basic salary and commission to the nearest peso?​pa help Naman guys please


Answer:

Jose's total income for the month including basic salary and commission is P67,600 to the nearest peso.

Step-by-step explanation:

First, we need to find the commission Jose gets on the first P300,000 of his personal sales. To do this, we multiply P300,000 by 3% which is P300,000 x 0.03 = P9,000.

Next, we need to find the commission he gets on the remaining P420,000 of his personal sales. To do this, we multiply P420,000 by 4.5% which is P420,000 x 0.045 = P18,900.

Then, we add the two commission amounts to get P9,000 + P18,900 = P27,900.

Now we need to calculate the override commission on the sales of the people under his supervision. To do this, we multiply P1,350,000 by 2% which is P1,350,000 x 0.02 = P27,000

Finally, we add the basic salary, personal commission and override commission to get the total income: P12,700 + P27,900 + P27,000 = P67,600.

Therefore, Jose's total income for the month including basic salary and commission is P67,600 to the nearest peso.

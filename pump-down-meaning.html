12. the long cylinder Bicycle pump has a pressure of 1.02 atm. the pump is pushed down until the volume of air becomes 517 ml and the pressure becomes 2,23 atm. what is the initial volume of air in the pump? ​


Given:

P₁ = 1.02 atm

P₂ = 2.23 atm

V₂ = 517 mL

Required:

V₁

Solution:

P₁V₁ = P₂V₂

V₁ = P₂V₂ / P₁

V₁ = (2.23 atm)(517 mL) / 1.02 atm

V₁ = 1130 mL

V₁ = 1130 mL

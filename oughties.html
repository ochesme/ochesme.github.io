6. Mary and Rose ought to buy some chocolates and candies.Mary paid php 198 for 6 bars of chocolates and 12 pieces of candies.Rose bought the same kinds of choxolates and candies but only paid less than php 100.Suppose each piece of candy costs php 4,how many bars of chocolates an pieces of candies could rose have bought


Answer:

Rose have bought 2 bars of chocolates and 12 pieces of candies.

Step-by-step explanation:

Money Problem

Let us analyze the problem.

What is asked?

Suppose each piece of candy costs Php 4, how many bars of chocolates and pieces of candies could rose have bought?

What are the given?

amount paid by Mary - Php 198

Mary's bars of chocolates - 6

Mary's pieces of candies - 12

amount paid by Rose - paid less than Php 100

cost of a piece of candy - Php 4

We have four steps to find the number of bars of chocolates and pieces of candies that Rose bought with the amount of Php 98.

Find the amount of 12 pieces of candies.Find the amount of one chocolate bar.Find the amount paid by Rose.Find how many bars of chocolates and pieces of candies that could be bought with the amount paid by Rose.

What is the number sentence?

1. Find the amount of 12 pieces of candies.

We know that a piece of candy is Php 4. To find the amount of 12 pieces of candies, multiply pieces of candies by the amount of each.

12 × 4 = N

2. Find the amount of one chocolate bar.

Once we know the cost of candies, we need to subtract it from the amount of Mary paid and divide the number by 6, as the number of chocolate bars.

(Php 198 - cost of candies) ÷ 6 = N

3. Find the amount paid by Rose.

It is Php 100 less than.

Php 198 - Php 100 = N

4. Find how many bars of chocolates and pieces of candies that could be bought with Php 98.

List the multiples of each number.

Solution:

Step 1:

12 × 4 = N

12 × 4 = 48

Cost of 12 candies is Php 48.

Step 2:

(Php 198 - cost of candies) ÷ 6 = N

(Php 198 - Php 48) ÷ 6 = N

Php 150 ÷ 6 = Php 25

One chocolate bar is Php 25.

Step 3:

Php 198 - Php 100 = Php 98

Rose paid Php 98.

Step 4:

We know that one bar of chocolate is Php 25 and a piece of candy is Php 4.

List multiples of each cost smaller than Php 98.

Multiples of 25

25 50 75

Multiples of 4

4 8 12 16 20 24 28 32 36 40 44 48 52

Now using each multiples, find the two numbers that when we add the answer is 98.

We came up with the answer 50 and 48.

50 + 48 = 98

Therefore, Rose have bought 2 bars of chocolates and 12 pieces of candies.

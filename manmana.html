Answer:

20

Explanation:

----------------------------------------------------------------------

[tex] \\ \large \mathcal{KATANUNGAN:} \\ [/tex]

Mayroong 5 mansanas sa bawat basket. kung mayroong 4 basket, ilan lahat ang mansanas?

-------------------------------------------------

[tex] \large \mathcal{KASAGUTAN:} \\ [/tex]

[tex] \sf \underline{➪ \: 20 \: \: na \: \: mansanas} \\ [/tex]

-------------------------------------------------

[tex] \\ \large \mathcal{SOLUSYON:} \\ [/tex]

[tex] \sf{ = 5 \times 4} \\ \sf{ = 20} \\ \\ \sf{or} \\ \\ \sf{ = 5 + 5 + 5 + 5} \\ \\ \sf{ = 20} \\ [/tex]

-------------------------------------------------

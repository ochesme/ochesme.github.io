10. Problem : Jurainy and Rayhana love to bake cookies Jurainy can bake 60 cookies in an hour, while Rayhana can bake 80 with in an hour. They started to bake at 6:30 a.m. And agreed to same number of cookies. 20. How many hours will Rayhana finish baking?A. 2B. 3C. 4D. 5Paki sagot nmn po


Rayhana will finish baking in 3 hours. Thus, the answer is letter B. To find the answer, find the LCM of the cookies that Jurainy and Rayhana can bake. To do this, list down the number of cookies that they can back every hour.

Jurainy:

1 hour - 60

2 hours - 120

3 hours - 180

4 hours - 240

Rayhana:

1 hour - 80

2 hours - 160

3 hours - 240

The LCM of 60 and 80 is 240 and Rayhana can finish baking in 3 hours.

Least Common Multiple

Multiple refers to the product of a number and any counting number. Least common multiple is the smallest multiple that numbers have in common. The short term for it is LCM. There are methods to find LCM.

Listing Method - This is the method where you need to list down the multiples of the given numbers.

Prime Factorization - This is the method where you need to give the prime factors of the given numbers, align the factors then multiply.

26. 5 men can finish baking cakes in 4 hours. How many men were needed to finish the same job in 30 minutes?​


5 men can finish baking cakes in 4 hours. How many men were needed to finish the same job in 30 minutes?

Given

5 men can finish baking in 4 hours

That means each of the 5 men has 48 minutes of work.

Solutions

4 hours = 240 minutes

240 minutes ÷ 30 minutes = 8

So a 30 minute work is 8 times more faster than 4 hours work of baking cake.

In order to finish a 30 minute baking, we need to increase the number of workers (5 men) 8 times larger also, so that a 30 minute baking becomes plausible or possible.

5 men × 8 = 40

ANSWER: 40 workers to finish a 30 minute work of baking cakes.

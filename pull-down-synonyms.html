20. Which of the following is the best synonym for "digestion"? answer choices breaking down building up formation respiration​


Answer:

Breaking down - Breaking down is the best synonym for digestion.

#CarryOnLearning

The best synonym for "digestion" is breaking down.

Digestion is the process by which the body breaks down food into smaller molecules that can be absorbed and used for energy and nutrition. This process occurs in the digestive system, and it involves the mechanical and chemical breakdown of food. "Breaking down" is the best synonym for "digestion" because it captures the idea of breaking food into smaller pieces or components. "Building up," "formation," and "respiration" are not synonyms for "digestion."

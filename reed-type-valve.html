Answer:A true needle valve uses a tapered needle in a tapered seat, so as to provide fine control over flow rate. The float valve uses a pointed needle against a square-edged seat, to give a positive shut-off. As the float relies on gravity, the chamber must be mounted vertically.

The Float and Needle

The floats pivot on a rod and, via the tang, open or close the needle valve, causing fuel to enter or not enter the chamber. When fuel is drawn up the main jet, the fuel level in the chamber drops, thus the float also drops.

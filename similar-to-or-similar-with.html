20. 19. In the figures at the right, if then is similar to by. What similarity theorem?A. SSS SimilarityB. SAS SimilarityC. ASA SimilarityD. SAA Similarity​


Answer:

SSS SIMILARITY THEOREM : If all three pairs of corresponding sides of two triangles are proportional, then the two triangles are similar

SAS SIMILARITY THEOREM : states that if two sides in one triangle are proportional to two sides in another triangle and the included angle in both are congruent, then the two triangles are similar.

ASA SIMILARITY THEOREM states that if two angles and the included side of one triangle are congruent to two angles and the included side of another triangle, then the two triangles are congruent.

SAA SIMILARITY THEOREM Angle-Angle-Side (AAS or SAA) Congruence Theorem: If two angles and a non-included side in one triangle are congruent to two corresponding angles and a non-included side in another triangle, then the triangles are congruent.

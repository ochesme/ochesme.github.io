11. Carlo prepared a sumptuous breakfast for us. active voice or passive voice​


Answer:

passive voice is the correct answer.

Answer:

Active Voice.

Explanation:

Because the active voice emphasizes the person or agent who performs an action, in short, the “actor.” The passive voice emphasizes the recipient of the action or sometimes the action itself.

If it was Passive Voice, it should've been "A sumptuous breakfast was prepared for us by Carlo". Always take note that if the sentence includes the word "was", it is most likely Passive Voice.

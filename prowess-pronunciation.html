27. elements of plot of the prowess of aliguyon


"The following are the elements of the plot of the story " The Prowess of Aliguyon" :

Exposition : Aliguyon was born. He was strong , precocious , handsoem and has a great skill in hunting and the use of spear.

Rising Action : Aliguyon became a man and talked to his friends about the war and fought for three year agaisnt Pumbakhayon

Climax : Pumbakhayon and Aliguyon realized that they were just wasting their time as noone has ever lost the fight and noone won so they decided to make a tie of peace.

Falling Action : There was a huge feast at the house of Pumbakhayon where Pangaiwan waited . There, Aliguyon found Bugan, the daughter of Pangaiwan. Aliguyon fell inlove with her in an instant and decided to ask for her hand.

Resolution : Bugan and Aliguyon got married and the ties of peace between Daligdigan and Hanangga grew stronger. Aliguyon and Pumbakhayon destroyed their enemy by making a friend.

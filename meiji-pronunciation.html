9. Ano ang meiji era????pa SG


Ang Meiji Period o Era ay ang panahon sa kasaysayan ng bansang Japan na sumasaklaw mula Oktubre 23, 1868 hanggang Hulyo 30, 1912. Isinunod ito sa pangalan ng emperador na namamahala noon na si Emperador Meiji, at sinundan naman siya ni Emperador Taishō, na tinawag naman na Taishō perid ang panahon ng kaniyang pamamahala. Noong panahong ng Meiji Period ang kapangyarihan ng emperador ay naisauli muli. Nagkaroon din ng pagbabago sa lipunan ng bansang Japan. Mula sa pagiging nakahiwalay sa impluwensiya ng kanluraning mga bansa at lipunang pyudal tungo sa modernisasyon at industriyalisasyon ng bansa at naging isa sa mga kilalang makapangyarihang bansa noon sa modernog daigdig. Naimpluwensiyahan ang pagbabago at mga reporma sa bansang Japan ng mga ideyang kanluranin pagdating sa siyensya, teknolohiya, pilosopiya, pulitika, at iba pa.

Mula noon inalis na ang sistema ng pag-uuri ng isa taong sa lipunan ayon sa kaniyang katayuan. Halimbawa ang mga samurai, na nakahanay sa mga warrior class ay maaaring maging mga magsasaka na lamang o pumasok sa industriya ng pakikipagkalakan o komersiyo, ang mga ordinaryong mga mamamayan ay maaari nang maging bahagi ng hukbong sandatahan ng bansang Japan. Nilayon noon na maging isang demokaratikong estado ang bansang Japan na may pantay-pantay na karapatan ang bawat isa.

Ang sistema ng edukasyon noon panahong iyon ay nireporma at isinunod sa sistema ng mga Pranses at nang maglaon sa sistema ng mga Aleman. Pagdating naman sa sektor ng militar, naging mataas ang priyoridad dito lalo na noong pagdating ng panahon ng imperyalismo.

10. what changes did emperor meiji enact


The Meiji Restoration was a paramount change in the structure of Japanese society from the top down that allowed Japan to heavily modernize in order to compete with European as well as American forces encroaching in the Pacific.

Personally, Emperor Meiji actually accomplished nothing. The primary force for this restoration was the Meiji Six Society along with the resulting Meiji oligarchy headed by Okubo Toshimichi.

To summarize what exactly Emperor Meiji's government did, here's a list I compiled of the various changes Meiji and his government enacted in order to modernize Japan:

Abolishment of the four-tier class system that had dominated Japan since the 11th CenturyForcing Samurai and Daimyo to join commoners, no longer would they hold title and privilege, land was taken and divided into prefecturesImplementation of European-style conscription into the newly formed Imperial ArmyEstablishment of the Imperial Army and Navy, western-style uniforms and heavy use of European firearms and tacticsEstablished Universal Elementary Education for both boys and girlsA dramatic industrial shift from a textile and agriculture based economy to one emphasizing heavy industry and weapons manufacturingPassing of the Meiji Constitution in 1899, modeled after Prussian-style of governmentSeizing the peninsula of Korea and defeating the Qing Dynasty in the Sino-Japanese War of 1894-1895Sent many Japanese technicians and scientists abroad to study under the various European nations as well as the United States. Education in France and Prussia, British help in naval building, Prussian help in building of troops and engineering of fortifications, influx of American capital, etc.Massive expansion of transportation and infrastructure systems throughout JapanEstablishment of the Bank of Japan as a result of spending during the Meiji Restoration as well as currency reformCreation of the Diet of JapanDefeated Russia in the Russo-Japanese War, gaining immense prestige

The Meiji Restoration transformed Japan in the span of a single generation from that of an isolated island power to a dominant Asian power by the turn of the 20th Century. She had the Army and Navy to dominate European powers as well as the rigid societal discipline needed for aggressive expansion into various regions of Asia. It cannot be understated how far-reaching the effects of the Meiji Restoration were.

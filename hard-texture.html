17. Give 5 examples of Rough, smooth, soft, hard textures


Examples of Rough, Smooth, Soft and Hard Textures

When talking about textures, textures are either the feel or appearance of a certain surface or a substance.

Rough Textures

These are textures that are uneven or coarse. In simple words, not having or less smooth texture and the opposite of smooth.

Examples of Rough Texture

PineappleCementSand PaperRoadLimestone

Smooth Textures

These are the textures that are not rough. Is it the opposite of rough. Have an even and regular surface or appearance.  It has no roughness, lumps, or holes. A surface free from roughness or bumps or ridges or irregularities or having a fine-grained structure.

Examples of Smooth Texture

SatinGlassJellyTomatoPaperSoft Textures

These are textures that are pleasant to touch or feel, not something that is rough or hard. Can be easily bent and have smooth curves.

Examples of Soft Texture

A Cotton pillow or anything that is made of cottonA baby blanketSoft fabricSilkAnimal fur especially cats and dogs fur

Hard Textures

These are textures that cannot easily break or bend. Opposite of soft texture. These are rigid, firm, solid and resistant to pressure.

Examples of Hard Texture

RockWoodPine TreesMetalSteel Bars

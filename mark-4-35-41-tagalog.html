9. what is a pattern this 1. 41, 39, 37, 35, 33,?


In mathematics, arithmetic progression also known as an arithmetic sequence is define as a sequence of numbers such that the two consecutive terms has a common difference.

Common difference means the second minus the first one.

Arithmetic Sequence FormulaFinding nth term: an= a1 + (n-1)dFinding sum: sn= 1/2n(a1+an)Wherean is the nth termsn is the sum of all termsa1 is the first termn is the number of termsd is the common difference

In the given problem, you need to identify its pattern.

Note: The pattern of arithmetic sequence is can be find in common difference.

The sequence:

41, 39, 37, 35, 33

Common difference (d) is equal to the difference of two consecutive term, a2 - a1.

d= a2 - a1, a2= 39, a1= 41

d= 39 - 41

d= -2

Therefore the pattern of the sequence is in every next term is reduced by 2.

18. 1) order 32, 37, 35, 34, 25, 41, 34 25, 32, 34, 34, 35, 37, 41 Mean Mode Median Range​


To find the mean, we add up all the numbers and divide by the total number of numbers:

32 + 37 + 35 + 34 + 25 + 41 + 34 + 25 + 32 + 34 + 34 + 35 + 37 + 41 = 450

There are 14 numbers in the list, so:

mean = 450/14 ≈ 32.14

To find the mode, we look for the number that appears most frequently in the list. In this case, there are two modes: 34 and 35, which both appear three times.

The median is the middle number when the numbers are arranged in order. First, we need to put the numbers in order:

25, 25, 32, 32, 34, 34, 34, 34, 35, 35, 37, 37, 41, 41

There are 14 numbers in the list, so the median is the average of the seventh and eighth numbers:

median = (34 + 34)/2 = 34

The range is the difference between the largest and smallest numbers in the list. The smallest number is 25 and the largest number is 41, so:

range = 41 - 25 = 16

Therefore, the mean is approximately 32.14, the mode is 34 and 35, the median is 34, and the range is 16.

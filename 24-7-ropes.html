17. Chad has a rope that is 15 yards long. How many pieces of rope measuring 5/7 of a yard can he divide his rope into?A. 28B. 21C. 35D. 14​


Answer: B.21 pieces

Chad has the ability to cut his rope into 21 parts.

We divide to solve.What we need to know is 15 divided by 5/7.It's the same as multiplying by the reciprocal when splitting fractions (flipped version of the fraction).

[tex]15 \div \frac{5}{7} = 15 \times \frac{7}{5} [/tex]

[tex] \frac{15}{1} \times \frac{7}{5} = \frac{105}{5} [/tex]

[tex] \frac{105}{5} =\red 2\red1[/tex]

Therefore, Chad can divide his rope in 21 pieces.

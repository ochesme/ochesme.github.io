2. Romeo and Juliet cast of story​


Answer:

The characters in William Shakespeare's play "Romeo and Juliet" include:

1. Romeo Montague: The young and passionate son of the Montague family who falls deeply in love with Juliet.

2. Juliet Capulet: The beautiful and innocent daughter of the Capulet family who falls in love with Romeo.

3. Lord Capulet: Juliet's father who is strict and protective of his daughter.

4. Lady Capulet: Juliet's mother who is more distant and less involved in her daughter's life.

5. Tybalt: Juliet's hot-headed cousin who hates the Montagues and is quick to fight.

6. Mercutio: Romeo's close friend who is witty and sarcastic.

7. Benvolio: Romeo's cousin who is more level-headed and tries to keep the peace.

8. Friar Laurence: A wise and compassionate priest who agrees to marry Romeo and Juliet in secret.

9. The Nurse: Juliet's loyal and bawdy caretaker who helps Juliet with her secret love for Romeo.

10. Prince Escalus: The ruler of Verona who tries to maintain peace between the feuding Montagues and Capulets.

11. Paris: A wealthy nobleman who wants to marry Juliet and is a rival to Romeo.

12. Montague and Lady Montague: Romeo's parents who are concerned about their son's melancholy behavior.

Explanation:

These characters interact in a complex web of love, hatred, and loyalty, leading to tragic consequences for Romeo and Juliet and their families.

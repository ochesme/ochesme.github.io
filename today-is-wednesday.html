5. if today is wednesday, then tomorrow is thursdaywhat: converse​


Answer:

Converse Statement: If tomorrow is Thursday, then today is Wednesday.

Step-by-step explanation:

Converse statement is formed by switching the hypothesis and the conclusion.

Given Conditional Statement: If today is Wednesday, then tomorrow is Thursday.

Hypothesis: Today is Wednesday.

Conclusion: Tomorrow is Thursday.

To write the converse statement, switch the hypothesis and conclusion from the conditional statement.

Converse Statement: If tomorrow is Thursday, then today is Wednesday.

13. if today is wednesday,what day was it 100 days ago?​


Answer:

100 days ago would be a multiple of 7 days (one week) plus 2 additional days (Wednesday to Monday is 5 days, and Monday to Sunday is 6 days).

100 days ÷ 7 days/week = 14 weeks and 2 days

So, 100 days ago was 14 weeks and 2 days ago from Wednesday, which was a Sunday.

28. what is inverse of today is tuesday,the next day is wednesday​


Answer:

The inverse of "today is Tuesday, the next day is Wednesday" is "today is Wednesday, the next day is Thursday."

Step-by-step explanation:

Inverse means to reverse or switch the order of elements in a statement. In this case, we are reversing the order of days in a statement about what day it is today and what day it will be tomorrow.

The original statement says "today is Tuesday, the next day is Wednesday." The inverse of this statement switches the order of the days: "today is Wednesday, the next day is Thursday."

So, the inverse of a statement about what day it is and what day it will be tomorrow is simply a switch of the current and next day. I hope this has answered your question. Please give a heart if it has. Thank you!

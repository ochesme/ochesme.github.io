3. what type of mixture is caffe latte?​


Answer:

Lattes (or caffe lattes) are espresso drinks made with a fairly large amount of steamed milk.

Explanation:

15. A mocha latte sells at a regular price of P180. Because the coffee shop has an anniversary treat today, they sell the latte for only P80. Find the percent of the decrease in the price.​


Answer:

The percent of the decrease in the price is 55.56%.

Step-by-step explanation:

Markdown = original price - new price

= 180 - 80

Markdown = 100

Markdown Rate = (Markdown ÷ Orig. Price) × 100

= ( 100 ÷ 180) × 100

Markdown Rate = 55.56%

24. what is the difference between cappuccino and caffe latte?​


Answer:

A cappuccino has an even distribution of espresso, steamed milk, and foamed milk. A latte has way more steamed milk and a light layer of foam. A cappuccino is distinctly layered, while in a latte the espresso and steamed milk are blended together.

Operations Contents

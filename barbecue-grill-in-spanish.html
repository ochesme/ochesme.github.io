> As it cooks it heats up and smokes, its gas escapes and becomes “Heat Energy”.

Electric fan - MECHANICAL

> It has its own motion or involvement of physical movement, it is called Mechanical.

> It heats up and its smoke comes out, it has gas coming out, so it's called "Heat energy"

> It has its own motion in accordance with “chemistry” and uses physical motion.

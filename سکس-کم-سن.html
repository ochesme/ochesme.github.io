2. What is 3×(×-5)=12 in standard form of quadraticکم​


Answer:

3x²-15x-12=0

Step-by-step explanation:

3x(x-5)=12

3x(x) + 3x(-5) = 12 Multiply 3x to the terms x & -5

3x² - 15x = 12

3x² - 15x -12 = 0 Transpose 12 to the other side

9. What can you say about this quote/saying save water save life?​


What I can say about the quote/saying "save water, save life" is that, as we all know, no one can live without water, and it is the greatest need of any living being. You should be aware of the significance of water in your life. "Save water, save life" means that we will be able to preserve life on our planet if we use our water resources wisely. Life can refer to any form of life, from plants to animals to humans.

Saving water means not wasting it; only using what we need and keeping the rest of the water pure and unpolluted. When we save water, we are doing it for both our generation and future generations.

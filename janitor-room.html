25. 7. The conference room received the afternoon sun. The room temperature was found to be 35.6 °C. The janitor turned on the air conditioner room. After a few minutes the thermometer recorded to be 25.3 "C. What was the estimated change in temperature?a. 12 °Cb. 11 °Cc. 10 °Cd. 9 °C​


ANSWER

C. 10°C

EXPLANATION

SINCE THE QUESTION IS THE ESTIMATED CHANGE IN TEMPERATURE WE CAN JUST SUBTRACT THE INITIAL TEMPERATURE WITH THE FINAL TEMPERATURE.

INITIAL TEMPERATURE: 35.6

FINAL TEMPERATURE: 25.3

35.6 - 25.3 = 10.3

*ESTIMATION RULE*

IF THE DECIMAL IS,

BELOW 5 = COPY THE WHOLE NUMBER

ABOVE 5 = ADD 1 IN THE WHOLE NUMBER

SO 10.3 BECOMES 10

25. 30. What is the measure of LD?​


Answer:

The deviation of haplotype frequencies from expected values based on gene frequencies is measured by D, which is a common estimate of LD. Different alleles should be randomly associated in a large population where mating is random. D values range from 0.25 to 0.25, with D = 0 indicating that no LD exists.

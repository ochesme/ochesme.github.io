5. paki explain po "children come into our life,stay and leave an imprint in our hearts "​


Answer:

The phrase "children come into our life, stay and leave an imprint in our hearts" is a way of expressing the profound impact that children can have on our lives. Children, whether they are our own or those of someone else, can have a significant effect on us that lasts long after they are no longer present in our daily lives.

When a child enters our life, they can bring joy, love, and laughter. We may watch them grow and learn, and feel proud of their accomplishments. They can also bring challenges, such as the responsibility of caring for them, guiding them, and making difficult decisions on their behalf. Through these experiences, we develop a bond with the child that can be incredibly strong.

Even when children leave our lives, such as when they grow up and move out of the house or when we lose them to illness or tragedy, they leave an imprint on our hearts. The memories of the time we spent with them, the lessons we learned, and the love we shared stay with us forever. These imprints can shape who we are and how we view the world.

In short, the phrase "children come into our life, stay and leave an imprint in our hearts" speaks to the transformative power of children on our lives. They can teach us, inspire us, and fill our hearts with love, leaving an indelible mark on us even after they are gone.

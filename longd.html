25. a fish is 75 cm long, the head is as long as the take if the head were twice as long and the tail were its present length the body would be 45 cm long, how long is each portion of the fish​


Head = 15 cm

Tail = 15 cm

Body = 45 cm

The formula to solve the problem, here they are:

Defined the variables for each part of the fish.

FISH = 75 cm (F)

Head = H

Tail = T

Body = 45 cm (B)

Make the variables become equations:

F = H + T + B

75 = H + T + 45

75 - 45 = H + T

30 = H + T

Because the problems said that HEAD = TAIL so, we just found one of the variables to get all the portion of the fish. To Find the T, just divide the equation by 2.

H = 30 ÷ 2

H = 15 cm

H = T

So, the Tail (T) is 15 cm also.

Hence, for each portion of the fish, the Head is 15 cm, the Body is 45 cm, and the Tail is 15 cm.

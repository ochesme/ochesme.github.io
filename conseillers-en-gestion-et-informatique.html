1. In triangle ENT, what is the included angle of line segment ET and segment EN?​


Answer:

The included angle is ∠ NET.

Step-by-step explanation:

Given:

ΔENT

Sides/Segment:

EN, NT, and ET

Included Angles:

(Clue: The common letter is the vertex of the included angle)

Between EN and NT: ∠N or ∠ENT

Between NT and ET: ∠T or ∠NTE

Between:  ET and EN: ∠E or ∠ NET

Theorem:  SAS (Side-Angle-Side)

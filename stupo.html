3. what is the relationship of stupas and the Buddhism​


Answer:

At its simplest, a stupa is a dirt burial mound faced with stone. In Buddhism, the earliest stupas contained portions of the Buddha's ashes, and as a result, the stupa began to be associated with the body of the Buddha. Adding the Buddha's ashes to the mound of dirt activated it with the energy of the Buddha himself.

14. Explain the significance of mosques, stupas, and temples in indian architecture


Answer:

Explanation:

What is the significance of religious architecture?

The history of architecture is concerned more with religious buildings than with any other type, because in most past cultures the universal and exalted appeal of religion made the church or temple the most expressive, the most permanent, and the most influential building in any community.

Significance of the mosque

They were spiritual, humanitarian and educational centres open to all people irrespective of their social, financial or racial status, or gender, thus emphasising the importance of equality for social progress.

Significance of the stupa

Stupas remind the Buddhist practitioner of the Buddha and his teachings almost 2,500 years after his death. For Buddhists, building stupas also has karmic benefits. Karma, a key component in both Hinduism and Buddhism, is the energy generated by a person's actions and the ethical consequences of those actions.

Significance of the temples

Hindu temple is a symbolic house, seat and body of divinity for Hindus. It is a structure designed to bring human beings and gods together, using symbolism to express the ideas and beliefs of Hinduism.

20. kahalagahan na ambag ng erope ang stuco​


Answer:

Ang Stucco o render ay isang materyal na konstruksyon na gawa sa mga pinagsama-samang, isang binder, at tubig. Ang Stucco ay inilapat na basa at tumigas sa isang napaka-siksik na solid. Ginagamit ito bilang isang pandekorasyon na patong para sa mga dingding at kisame, panlabas na panghaliling tabi ng gusali, at bilang isang eskultura at artistikong materyal sa arkitektura. Maaaring gamitin ang Stucco upang masakop ang hindi gaanong nakakaakit na mga kagamitang konstruksyon, tulad ng metal, kongkreto, cinder block, o luwad na brick at adobe.

Sa English, ang "stucco" minsan ay tumutukoy sa isang patong para sa labas ng isang gusali at "plaster" sa isang patong para sa loob; tulad ng inilarawan sa ibaba, ang materyal mismo ay madalas na kakaiba. Ang iba pang mga wikang European, kapansin-pansin kasama ang Italyano, ay walang parehong pagkakaiba; Ang stucco ay nangangahulugang plaster sa Italyano at nagsisilbi para sa pareho.

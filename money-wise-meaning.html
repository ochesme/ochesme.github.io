5. be wise in using your money to have enough savings. what does it mean? hoe it is related to the financial fitness?


Answer:

A general definition of financial fitness is to have the money you need, when you need it. A more detailed definition would include living within your means, not being in debt and to have savings.

It affects how you approach money, the way you view and use debt, how you think about your future, and how you view the financial habits of others. When you know how you approach money, you'll be more equipped to make intentional decisions that push you in a positive direction.

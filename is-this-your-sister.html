25. You have Php60 and your sister has Php120. You are saving Php7 per week and your sister is saving 5 per week. How long will it be before you and your sister have a same amount of money?​


Let X be a number of weeks, than you have

(60 + 7 * X) - ( 120 + 5 * X) = 0

Solve for X and you get X = 30 weeks

The key here is to recognize that when they both have equal amounts the difference between their amounts  will be 0.

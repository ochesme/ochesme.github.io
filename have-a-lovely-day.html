23. Zaijan and Loyd love to play basketball. Zaijan plays every 3rd day, Loyd plays every 6th day. When will they have a game on the same day?​


Answer:

✓ The answer is on the sixth day.

[tex]\large{\mathtt{Question:}}[/tex]

Zaijan and Loyd love to play basketball. Zaijan plays every 3rd day, Loyd plays every 6th day. When will they have a game on the same day?

[tex]\large{\mathtt{Solution:}}[/tex]

✓ In order to know the day both Zaijan and Loyd will play a game, you need to identify a number that is both multiple of 3 and 6.

[tex]3 = 3, 6, 9, 12, 15, 18, 21, 24... \\ 6=6, 12, 18, 24... [/tex]

✓ The lowest number that is both a multiple of 3 and 6 is simply, 6. Therefore, the answer is 6.

✓ The next days they'll play game together/on the same day will be on the 12th day then 18th and then 24th day and so on. They'll play games every sixth day.

Question:

Mary is 24 yrs old. Mary is twice as old as Merly was when Mary was as old as Merly is now. How old is Merly now?


Answer:

16 years old


Step-by-step explanation:

Let Merly be x now


When Mary was x, Mary was twice as old as Merly

⇒ Merly was 1/2x then


Find the difference in age:

Difference = x - 1/2x = 1/2x


Information we know:

Mary is 24 years old now

Mery is x years old now

Merly is 1/2 x younger than Mary


Solve x:

24 - x = 1/2x

3/2 x = 24

x = 24 ÷ 3/2

x = 16 years old


Answer: Merly is 16 years old now.

12. Bringing the dolls poem by merlie alunan


The poem bringing the doll written by Merlie Alunan is about a mother's realization that in one's moving on, one need not bring only those considered important. The predominant image of the poem is the doll. It can initially be found in the first 3 lines, which describr the doll as mangled.


Two dolls in rags and tatters,
one missing an arm and a leg,
the other blind in one eye—I grabbed them from her arms,
“No,” I said, “they cannot come.”Each tight baggage
I had packed
only for the barest need:
no room for sentiment or memory
to clutter with loose ends
my stern resolve. I reasoned,
even a child must learn
she cannot take what must be left behind.And so the boat turned seaward,
a smart wind blowing dry
the stealthy tears I could not wipe.
Then I saw—rags, tatters and all—
there among the neat trim packs,
the dolls I ruled to leave behind.Her silence should have warned me
she knew her burdens
as I knew mine:
her clean white years unlived—
and paid my price.
She battened on a truth
she knew I too must own:
when what’s at stake
is loyalty or love,
hers are the true rights.
Her own faiths she must keep, not I.

19. Two years ago, Perla was 7 times as old as Merly. Two years from now, Perla will be 6 years more than 4 times as old as Merly will be. Find the present age of each?


Let 'x' be Perla's age at present
      'y' be Merly's age at present
-------------------------------------------------------
Two years ago their ages were:
x - 2   ----Perla
y - 2   ----Merly
-------------------------------------------------------
Two years from now their ages will be:
x + 2  ----Perla
y + 2  ----Merly
-------------------------------------------------------
From the statement, 'two years ago Perla was 7 times as old as Merly'
x - 2 = 7(y - 2)
x - 2 = 7y - 14
transpose -2 to the right
x = 7y - 14 + 2
x = 7y - 12    -----equation 1
------------------------------------------------------
From the statement, 'two years from now Perla will be 6 years more than 4 times as old as Merly'
x  + 2 = 4(y+2) + 6
x + 2 = 4y + 8 + 6
transpose 2 to the right
x = 4y + 8 + 6 - 2
x = 4y + 12   -----equation 2
------------------------------------------------------
equate equations 1 and 2
7y - 12 = 4y + 12
7y - 4y = 12 + 12
3y = 24
y = 8
------------------------------------------------------
Substitute y=8 to equation 1
x = 7y - 12
x = 7(8) - 12
x = 56 - 12
x = 44
Therefore Perla is 44 yrs old while Merly is 8 yrs old.

24. what is adverbs of manner, adverbs of degree, adverbs of place, adverbs of time, and adverbs of frequency pa help po brainlest ko agad english


Answer:

Adverbs of manner

-are used to tell the way in which somethings happend. They mainly modify verbs and are formed by adding -ly to their equivalent adjectives.

Example

''What number are calling, Madam?'' the operator asked patiently

Adverbs of degree

-modify verbs, adjectives or other adverbs by showing their intensity or extent.

Example

After repacking the relief goods, the volunteer worker was exhausted completely.

Adverbs of place

Tell where something happens such as above everywhere, here, nowhere and there

Example

People have started to work from home almost everywhere

Adverbs of time

-identify when the verb took place

Example

The new normal nowadays is staying at home, wearing mask and observing social distancing

Adverbs of frequency

-explain how often the verb occurs

Example

Department of health constantly reminds the public that washing of hands is the best way to prevent contagion due to the virus

26. where do critical essays find its place in this modern society?sana ma sagot agad po in english ​


Answer:

A critical essay is a form of academic writing in which a writer evaluates and analyzes a text. It can be a book, article, or movie, etc.

In this type of writing, the main objective is not to convince your audience but to create an informative analysis.

You have to come up with your interpretation and prove with facts or evidence from other sources of work.

Teachers assign this type of assignment to challenge the critical analyzing ability of students. They want to get a well-written paper with clear arguments and reliable references to support the claim.

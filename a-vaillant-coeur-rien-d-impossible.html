3. Give a scenario that suggest Autocratic, Democratic and Free Rien Leadership.​


Autocratic Leadership Scenario:

An autocratic leader makes all the decisions without consulting their subordinates. For instance, a CEO who does not consider the opinions and ideas of the other company executives when making a decision about the company's direction can be an example of autocratic leadership. The CEO is the only one making decisions that affect the company, and no one else's input is taken into consideration.

Democratic Leadership Scenario:

A democratic leader is someone who encourages collaboration, participation, and open communication. For example, a teacher who leads a group discussion in which everyone is given equal opportunity to contribute to the conversation and the final decision is made based on the majority vote of the group is a good example of democratic leadership.

Free Reign Leadership Scenario:

A free reign leader gives their subordinates maximum freedom and minimal guidance, allowing them to operate independently without much supervision. For instance, a software development company that allows its employees to work from home and set their own schedules, as long as they meet their project deadlines and produce high-quality work, can be an example of free reign leadership. The employees are given the freedom to work in a way that suits them best, without any micromanagement.

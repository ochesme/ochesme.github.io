Explanation:

In the subject-verb agreement, a singular subject takes singular verb and a plural subject takes plural verb

Here's the rule:

1. A subject will come before a phrase beginning with Of. This is the key rule. Just like the question in number 1 that is : the followers of the movie the subject here is THE FOLLOWERS and it is in plural form so we should take plural verb STUDY.

2. Two si gular subjects connected by OR, EUTHER/OR, NEUTHER/NOR require a sibgular verb such as in the case of question number 5. " the best cinematography or story" which is in singular form so the best verb to use should also be in singular form.

3. However when two sibgular subjects are connected by AND, they will become plural because AND indicates two which is plural in NUMBER. SO here in the case of the question number 3 subject is the best director and scriptwriter which take plural form so we will use plural verb (remove "s" in the verb.
